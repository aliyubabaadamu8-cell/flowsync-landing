What FlowSync does

Built for the way distributed teams actually work

Not another task manager. FlowSync is the operating layer between your calendar, your chat, and your people.

Focus Time Protection

FlowSync reads your team's calendar and automatically blocks off uninterrupted work windows. Slack goes quiet. Meetings bounce. Everyone knows not to disturb.

  • Smart calendar scanning
  • Auto do-not-disturb across tools
  • Shared focus-time visibility

Async Status Sync

Every team member gets a live status card — what they're working on, when they're available, and what's blocked. No more "quick syncs" just to find out where things stand.

Flow Analytics

Weekly reports that show exactly how your team's time is spent — and where focus is being stolen. Data you can actually act on, not just stare at.

Integration Layer

Works with the stack you already use. Slack, Google Calendar, Notion, Linear, GitHub — FlowSync sits quietly in the background connecting them.

Meeting Trim Tool

Before any meeting lands on your calendar, FlowSync checks whether it should be a doc instead. Fewer meetings. The ones that survive actually matter.

Privacy by Default

Your team's work is yours. No training on your data. SOC 2 Type II certified. GDPR compliant. The admin controls are clear and you can audit everything.
